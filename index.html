Wenyu Zhu 朱文钰

PhD Applicant in AI for Science (Biology & Drug Discovery)

I am passionate about applying machine learning to advance drug discovery and molecular design. My research focuses on protein-ligand interaction prediction, structure-based virtual screening, and interpretable AI systems for biomedicine.

Research Interests

Machine Learning for Molecular Science

Multimodal representation learning and structure-based generative modeling for drug discovery

Protein-Ligand Interactions

Developing robust and interpretable models for binding affinity prediction and virtual screening

AI-Driven Drug Discovery

Integrating computational modeling with experimental validation for therapeutic development

Large Language Models

Applying LLMs to enhance interpretability and reasoning in molecular optimization
